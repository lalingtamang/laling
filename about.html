About Us

Resting on a serene hillock in Phedi Patan, the Sunshine Resort Pokhara offers a tranquil escape with panoramic 360-degree views of the Annapurna mountain range. This boutique property, established in early 2019, boasts spacious and vibrant rooms, a welcoming and well-trained staff, and hygienically prepared, tasty cuisine.

Guests can enjoy amenities such as an outdoor and indoor pool, spa services, a winery, and dining options including a restaurant and poolside bar. While slightly removed from the city center, the resort provides a shuttle service to Lakeside and complimentary airport/bus park pickup and drop-off, making it a peaceful retreat with convenient access to Pokhara's attractions.

Activities

At Sunshine Resort, we offer a wide range of activities and amenities to make your stay truly memorable. Some of our offerings include:

  • Spa and Wellness: Indulge in relaxing massages and other spa treatments.
  • Swimming: Enjoy a refreshing swim in the outdoor or indoor pool.
  • Vehicle Rental: Easily arrange transportation for exploring the surrounding areas.
  • Outdoor Sports: Engage in various outdoor recreational activities.
  • Cycling: Explore the local scenery at your own pace with cycling opportunities.
  • Bonfire: Gather around a cozy bonfire in the evenings.
  • Winery: Discover and enjoy the resort's on-site winery.
  • Poolside Bar: Relax and sip on beverages by the poolside.
